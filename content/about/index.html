Where to Find Our Products

St. Johns Farmers Market





The farm recently finished it's fifth season selling vegetables at the St. Johns Farmers Market. St. Johns is a wonderful, neighborhood market that is well loved and well supported by Northwest Portland! The market has a great variety of food products including 5+ farms, bread/baked goods, mushrooms, meat, eggs, plus many value-added products and ready to eat options.

Location: Corner of N Charleston and N Central in St. Johns

2025 Market Season:
Ever Saturday from 9 AM to 2 PM from May 3 to November 22, 2025.
Christmas market: December 13, 10 AM to 2 PM.

Clatskanie Food Hub





The Clatskanie Food Hub is a wonderful little storefront, event space, and organizational hub of Oregon's North Coast food community. They carry a wide variety of products from over 70 local vendors. The market is located roughly halfway between Portland and Astoria Oregon. Stop in to stock up for your next trip to the Oregon Coast.

Location: 80 NE Art Steele St. Clatskanie Oregon
Hours: Thursday 2-6 PM & Friday through Sunday 10 AM to 6 PM
